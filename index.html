Professional

Over the past 3 years I have worked in digital media, fine-tuning my skills in everything from campaign management to tracking budgets to campaign performance. During that time I’ve also gathered more technical skills such as tag management and data binding implementation.

At my core, I am creative-minded while also thinking logically on the steps needed to find solutions, which is how I got into front-end web development. I’m self-taught in front-end languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript after 100’s of hours of bootcamps, tutorials, and personal projects. I am also familiar with some libraries and frameworks like Bootstrap, jQuery, Vue.js, and learning more each day.

Personal

I’m just a regular guy in the neighborhood. I drink enough coffee to qualify as a “caffeine overdose” on WebMD, and if given the opportunity I WILL give your dog a belly rub. In my free time I write and perform standup comedy, snowboard, kickbox, and rewatch the Dinner Party episode of The Office.
